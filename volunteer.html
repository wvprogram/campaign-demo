Volunteer

We're a People-Powered campaign and need You! Want to change the East Side with me? Sign up to volunteer!

No experience is necessary!

We welcome you to join our campaign in caring for your community.

We welcome you to bring friends, neighbors, family members, children, and pets.

We know that change only happens when people join in community. We need people in office who are ready to do politics differently. This means governing with the people and organizing together to make sure that nobody's left behind.

Here's how you can take action with us:

Door Knocking: Meet with our neighbors at their homes or in community and share what issues matter to us most in East Side.

Phone Banking: Call our neighbors and share what issues matter to us most in East Side.
